Cinnamon Apple Dump Cake

Description:

Want to keep coming back to a great dump cake? This cinnamon apple dump cake is such a nice cake and so easy to make, and its a great way to use up extra apples. Top it with some vanilla ice cream, its SO GOOD!.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans apple pie filling
  • 8 oz of chopped pecans
  • Caramel Sauce
  • 1 box cinnamon toast crunch cake mix
  • 2 sticks unsalted butter

Cooking Steps

  1. Using a 9x13 size cake pan, spread the apple pie filling on the bottom of the pan.
  2. Squeeze the caramel sauce onto the applie pie filling.
  3. Next, add a layer of chopped pecans into the pan making sure you spread them evenly.
  4. Cover the spread with cake mix, and although very tempting, DO NOT STIR!
  5. Melt the butter sticks, and cover the layers with warmed butter.
  6. Lastly, place the pan in the oven at 375 degrees for approximately 45 minutes, or until a golden brown is achieved.
